# MVP Small Clinic

## Project Vision

MVP Small Clinic is a lightweight AI-powered appointment booking platform designed specifically for small private clinics with 1–2 doctors.

The primary objective is not to build a large healthcare SaaS platform.

The objective is:

* Launch quickly
* Acquire first paying clinics
* Keep infrastructure simple
* Minimize operational costs
* Validate market demand

---

## Target Customers

* Dental Clinics
* Cosmetology Clinics
* Psychologists
* Massage Clinics
* Family Doctors
* Small Private Medical Practices

Typical size:

* 1–2 doctors
* 1 receptionist (optional)
* Less than 100 appointments per day

---

## Core Product Value

Patient can:

* Find doctor
* Find service
* View available slots
* Book appointment
* Receive confirmation

Doctor can:

* Manage schedule
* View appointments
* Receive notifications

---

## MVP Principles

1. Simplicity over flexibility
2. Revenue before features
3. Single-clinic architecture
4. No multi-tenancy
5. No patient registration
6. AI assists booking only
7. Every feature must support customer acquisition
